HConnect1 — Remote Patient Monitoring Dashboard

Nytila Labs · Northeastern University Capstone · 2025–2026

A full-stack, PIPEDA/PHIPA-compliant web-based remote patient monitoring platform built for Canadian healthcare providers. The system allows clinical staff to monitor patient vital signs, manage alerts, coordinate care, and communicate securely. Supports four user roles: Doctor, Nurse, Administrator, and Patient.

The platform automatically compares each vital sign reading against clinically sourced thresholds and generates alerts. It tracks seven health metrics per patient — wellness, nutrition, stress, physical activity, depression risk, obesity, and movement — scored on a 0 to 1000 scale with trend indicators. Built and tested by a five-person team with 151 automated tests, all passing at submission.
